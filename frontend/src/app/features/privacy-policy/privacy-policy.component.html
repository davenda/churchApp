3. Text Message Program

3.1 Consent

By providing your phone number and opting into our text message program, you expressly consent to receive:

  • Weekly meeting reminders
  • Schedule changes and updates
  • Study materials and resources
  • Important announcements
  • Spiritual content and devotionals

3.2 Message Frequency

  • Regular meeting reminders (1-2 times per week)
  • Occasional announcements (2-3 times per month)
  • Schedule changes (as needed)
  • Program updates (as needed)

3.3 Text Message Rates

Message and data rates may apply. Consult your wireless carrier plan for details about your text messaging rates.

3.4 Opting Out

You may opt-out of text messages at any time by:

  • Replying "STOP" to any message
  • Emailing support@beteyared.com
  • Updating your preferences in your account settings

3.5 Help

For help with the text message program:

  • Reply "HELP" to any message
  • Email support@beteyared.com
